Ignite your appetite with our delicious flavours

Western Suites Hotel offers guests a delightful blend of local and international flavours. Start your day with a hearty breakfast at the stylish Fringale Restaurant & Bar, which also serves up delicious lunches and dinners in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. It's the perfect place to enjoy good food and great conversation with family, friends, or colleagues.
For a more casual experience, Rendezvous Bar & Tea Lounge is open all day, offering everything from morning coffee to afternoon tea and evening cocktails. Whether you're catching up with friends or just unwinding, it's a comfortable space to relax.
If you're in the mood for music, head to KTV - Karaoke Bar, where you can sing your heart out with a wide selection of popular songs and classic show tunes.
At Western Suites Hotel, there's something for everyone — from fine dining to fun nights out and everything in between.


Central located in Tashkent

Tashkent is the sprawling capital of Uzbekistan and is one of the oldest cities on the Great Silk Road between China and Europe. This garden city is incredibly green and tastefully combines medieval buildings with elegant European architecture from the time of the Turkestan governorship, concrete ‘blocks’ from the Soviet era and the modern sparkling high-rise commercial buildings.
Western Suites Hotel is a chic urban 3-star centrally located in the Kichik Beshagach Street in Yakkasaroy district, in Tashkent, the Capital of Uzbekistan. Kichik Beshagach Street lies in the southern part of the city and links many big and main streets including Nukus, Shakhrizabz and Mirabad street. Situated merely 2 km (6 min) from Tashkent International Airport, Western Suites Hotel is ideal for leisure and business travellers.
