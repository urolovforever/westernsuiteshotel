Our Amenities

Western Suites Hotel

Western Suites Hotel offers guests a delightful blend of local and international flavours.
Start your day with a hearty breakfast at the stylish Fringale Restaurant & Bar, which also serves up delicious lunches and dinners in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. It's the perfect place to enjoy good food and great conversation with family, friends, or colleagues.
For a more casual experience, Rendezvous Bar & Tea Lounge is open all day, offering everything from morning coffee to afternoon tea and evening cocktails. Whether you're catching up with friends or just unwinding, it's a comfortable space to relax.
If ybrou're in the mood for music, head to KTV - Karaoke Bar, where you can sing your heart out with a wide selection of popular songs and classic show tunes.
At Western Suites Hotel, there's something for everyone — from fine dining to fun nights out and everything in between.

Our Restaurant

At Western Suites Hotel, Fringale Restaurant & Bar offers a delicious mix of local and international cuisine. From hearty breakfasts to flavorful lunches and relaxing dinners, guests can enjoy freshly prepared meals in a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're dining with family, friends, or colleagues, Fringale is the perfect place to enjoy great food and good company.

Our Karaoke Bar

KTV - Karaoke Bar at Western Suites Hotel is the perfect place to let loose and have fun. With a wide selection of songs, from classic hits to today’s chart-toppers, guests can sing their hearts out in a lively and friendly atmosphere. Whether you're celebrating with friends or just in the mood for a night of music and laughter, KTV - Karaoke Bar promises a great time for everyone.

Our Gourmandise Room

Our conference room is fully equipped to make your meetings as comfortable as possible.
Gourmandise at Western Suites Hotel offers a delightful selection of freshly made pastries, light snacks, and refreshing beverages in a cozy, inviting setting. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee on the go, enjoying a leisurely afternoon tea, or meeting friends for a casual lunch, Gourmandise provides the perfect blend of flavor and comfort. With its relaxed atmosphere and tempting treats, it’s the ideal spot to unwind at any time of day.

Massage

For those seeking relaxation, guests can enjoy an authentic Thai massage at a nearby facility. While this service is not operated by the hotel, it offers a perfect way to unwind after a long day. Please note that guests who wish to use this service will need to pay separately.

Korean Restaurant

Guests can also indulge in authentic Korean cuisine at a nearby restaurant. While not operated by the hotel, this dining option offers a fantastic selection of traditional Korean dishes, perfect for those looking to explore bold flavors and rich culinary traditions. Please note that guests who wish to dine there will need to pay separately.
