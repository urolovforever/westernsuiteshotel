About Western Suites Hotel

Western Suites Hotel is a modern, deluxe hotel located at 96, Kichik Beshyagach Street in Yakkasaroy District, right in the heart of Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan. Our prime location offers easy access to major streets like Shakhrizabz and Mirabad, ensuring quick connections to the city’s top business centers, shopping areas, and cultural landmarks.
Just 8 kilometers (about 20 minutes) from Tashkent International Airport, Western Suites Hotel is perfectly suited for both business and leisure travelers looking for comfort, convenience, and modern elegance.
Our contemporary design, affordable luxury, and personalized service create a welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Whether you’re here for work or relaxation, you’ll enjoy thoughtfully designed rooms, upscale amenities, and a warm hospitality experience.
The hotel also features excellent dining options, comfortable lounge areas, and modern facilities, ensuring every guest feels at home while exploring the beautiful city of Tashkent.
